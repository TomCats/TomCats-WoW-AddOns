Frequently Asked Questions about Discoveries

|cFFFFD100Q:|r What is a discovery?

|cFFFFD100A:|r Currently, these are any rare spawn/encounter or map icon type that TomCat's Tours is not yet aware of. What can be discovered will change according to what features are being worked on.


|cFFFFD100Q:|r Can I see what I've discovered before I send it to you?

|cFFFFD100A:|r Yes! The discovery is base64 encoded (not encrypted) for ease of copy/paste. There are a number of tools and websites available to view what is in base64 encoded text, including base64decode.org


|cFFFFD100Q:|r I'm making a lot of discoveries. Can I disable this?

|cFFFFD100A:|r No need. As the community submits their discoveries, each update to the addon will prevent anyone from discovering them again. This will sort out on its own fairly quickly.
